## Defining the Dimension

Vocabulary autonomy captures the degree to which a subject generates its own original language for describing internal states versus adopting the interviewer's offered terms. The spectrum runs from fully reactive/adopted vocabulary to fully self-generated, distinctive, personal language that resists or transcends offered framings.
